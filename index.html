01 About Me

Hi, I'm Seungwon Cho, an undergraduate at Seoul National University majoring in Chemistry with an interdisciplinary major in System Semiconductor Engineering for AI.

I'm drawn to the chemistry of functional materials — understanding how structure controls properties, and how that knowledge can be applied to catalysis and to the semiconductor materials that power modern AI hardware. I enjoy connecting fundamental chemistry with real engineering problems across these two fields.
